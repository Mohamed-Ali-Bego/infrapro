About Us


Company Overview :

We are a pioneer in the field of low current systems, integrated systems contracting, and integrated home control through smart homes. We have proudly been serving the Egyptian market since 2017. We specialize in providing integrated solutions designed to meet the diverse needs of small and medium-sized businesses and large projects. Our business extends to include sales, installation and support of large-scale security and protection systems, in addition to providing telecommunications products.

Our Business :

INFRATECH company works in difficult market environments and despite frequent price changes , our company tries to fix the price with the customer for the maximum period, and gives priority to the highly efficient product. And provide the products required by the owner according to the types known to him or nominated by our company. We are committed to providing specially designed information technology solutions.

Our Objective :

our goal is to provide the best service to customers, achieve interconnection between our company and customers, and meet all the customer’s requirements in terms of systems so that he does not need any other company to intervene in the project and his dealings with our company are guaranteed by our company.

Integration Methodology :

Understanding the varied demands of different sectors , we deliver a wide array of solutions. We dissect the larger picture into components, addressing each individually, and assigning different aspects to the various divisions within INFRATECH. Our employees receive direct training from product vendors, ensuring deep familiarity with a broad range of products . This approach allows us to seamlessly integrate different products and platforms to meet diverse needs.
